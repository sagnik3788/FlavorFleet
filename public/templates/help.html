Help for Customers

  • Is extra discount applicable on item total?

    • ▼

    Extra discount is applicable on the effective item total, which is the item total after the coupon discount has been applied.

  • Is Flavorfleet One available in all cities?

    • ▼

    Currently Flavorfleet One is available in select cities only.

  • Do you charge for delivery?

    • ▼

    Delivery fee varies from city to city and is applicable if the order value is below a certain amount. Additionally, certain restaurants might have fixed delivery fees. Delivery fee (if any) is specified on the 'Review Order' page.

  • Can I edit my order?

    • ▼

    In order to edit your order, please click on "Help" and then "I want to modify items in my order." We will connect you to a support agent who will assist you with the same. Please note that your order can be edited only if the restaurant is yet to confirm your order. Post that, we may not be able to modify your order if food preparation has started.

  • I want to cancel my order

    • ▼

    In order to cancel your order, please click on "Help" and then "I want to cancel my order." Please note that we may charge you a cancellation fee as it helps us to minimize food wastage and also compensate our restaurant partners for canceled orders.
